About

Greetings, World! I'm Daniel Aceituno, a dedicated software developer on a mission to leverage code for positive change. I'm passionate about creating innovative solutions that empower people and organizations to thrive in the digital age.

With a background in computer engineering and three years of self-taught coding experience, I thrive on the dynamic challenges that technology presents. Currently employed as an IT consultant and data analyst, I'm driven by a relentless pursuit of knowledge and growth in the ever-evolving landscape of software development.

My current focus lies in expanding my expertise in web development, with aspirations to evolve into a proficient full-stack developer. Simultaneously, I'm honing my skills in C++ through personal projects, fueled by a passion to realize my dream of delving into game development.

When I'm not coding, you can find me indulging in my hobbies of drawing, playing the guitar, and playing video games. I'm also an avid reader and a passionate advocate for mental health awareness.

Feel free to reach out to me via email or connect with me on LinkedIn. I'm always open to new opportunities and collaborations!
